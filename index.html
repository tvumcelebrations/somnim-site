Philosophy

Pollution exposure is constant. Your skin remains unprotected.

In urban environments, we have learned to protect our lungs. Masks filter the air we breathe. Air purifiers clean interior spaces. But our skin—the body's largest organ—remains continuously exposed to particulate matter, UV radiation, and environmental oxidative stress.

somnim was developed as a functional response to this daily reality. Not as cosmetic skincare. Not as a wellness ritual. But as a daily defence layer designed for people who live and work in polluted cities.

The principle is simple

Your skin encounters environmental aggressors every day. Pollution particles settle on the surface. UV rays penetrate deeper layers. Oxidative stress accelerates cellular damage. These are measurable, constant factors—not occasional concerns.

somnim provides a physical and chemical barrier. It hydrates to maintain the skin's natural protective function. It contains broad-spectrum sun protection. It creates a surface layer that reduces direct contact with particulate pollution.

What this is not

This is not anti-aging skincare making transformative promises. This is not luxury beauty with marketing narratives about glow, radiance, or youth. This is functional skin protection formulated with the same principles applied to respiratory protection: reduce exposure, maintain barrier integrity, support daily defence.

For cities. For daily use. For anyone who recognizes that environmental exposure requires a practical response.

the product

Daily Skin Defence

A single formulation designed for daily application in urban environments with high pollution exposure. Packaged in frosted glass with a pump dispenser. 50ml.

What it does

  • Hydration barrier — Maintains skin moisture to support the natural protective barrier function
  • Broad-spectrum sun protection — SPF 30 mineral-based UV defence for daily exposure
  • Pollution contact reduction — Creates a surface layer that limits particulate matter adherence to skin
  • Antioxidant support — Contains vitamin E to address oxidative stress from environmental exposure

What it does not claim

This product is not a medical treatment. It does not claim to reverse aging, eliminate wrinkles, or transform skin appearance. It does not claim to detoxify, purify, or deeply cleanse. It is not a cosmetic solution to aesthetic concerns.

Daily Skin Defence is a functional barrier product. It reduces environmental contact and supports the skin's existing protective mechanisms. The goal is protection, not transformation.

Application

Apply to clean, dry skin each morning. One pump covers the face and neck. Reapply if spending extended time outdoors or after washing. Use consistently for continuous protection.

Lightweight, non-greasy formula

The cream texture is designed for daily wear under or without makeup. It absorbs quickly, leaves no white cast, and doesn't interfere with other skincare steps. The formulation sits comfortably on skin throughout the day without feeling heavy or occlusive.

Formulation notes

Mineral-based UV filters. Fragrance-free. Free from parabens, phthalates, and synthetic colorants. Formulated to be non-comedogenic and suitable for daily use across skin types. Developed with dermatological input. Not tested on animals.
